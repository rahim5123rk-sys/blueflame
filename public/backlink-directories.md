## Tips for Getting Backlinks from Other Sites

1. **Write guest posts** — Offer to write a gas safety tip article for local letting agent blogs, property management sites, or community websites in Worcestershire.

2. **Partner with letting agents** — Offer a referral arrangement with local letting agents (Worcestershire Property, Connells, etc.) who manage properties and need regular CP12s. Ask them to mention you on their website.

3. **Local press** — The Worcester News and Worcestershire Life sometimes run features on local businesses. Reach out with a story angle (e.g., "local heating engineer with 5-star rating helps landlords stay compliant").

4. **Your Gas Safety Guide page** — Share the URL `https://www.blueflameworcester.com/gas-safety-guide` with local landlord forums, Facebook groups (e.g., "Worcestershire Landlords"), and letting agent newsletters. Useful content gets shared and linked to naturally.

5. **Sponsor a local event** — Sponsoring a local community event in Worcester often earns a backlink from the event's website.

Once you've submitted to directories, track your progress with free tools:

- **Moz Link Explorer (free)** — Similar to Ahrefs
